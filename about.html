About us

About Kalinda IT

Kalinda IT Services Pty Ltd, a 100% indigenously owned Australian IT services business. Formed in 2018 with headquarters in Australia, Kalinda IT Services has achieved a significant benchmark in IT services and system integration.


Our Executive Team has extensive experience across technology and supporting customers through the continuous journey of digital transformation, ensuring that every dollar invested in IT delivers the maximum return. Kalinda IT Services team is committed to outperform competition and stay abreast to the innovation curve.


We have empowered our clients across APAC by offering innovative, cost-effective, end-to-end business solutions and services that are customizable, dynamic and extensible. We deliver a full range of services including systems integration, managed services, custom platform builds and software application development.


We are Supply Nation Certified.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the technology partner of choice for forward looking customers which helps them to innovate and build competitive advantage with operational efficiency.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower organisations to take advantage of the latest technology and services to overcome their business challenges in the most efficient way.

Our Quality Policy

We know what makes IT services and projects successful — and our clients benefit from the high-quality standards we’ve set for ourselves. Our consultants, project managers, system engineers and technicians provide tool-based management to ensure that we deliver exactly what our customers need.


We ensure high quality standards through:

  • Certifications: We are backed by top-level certifications: ITIL, PRINCE2 and ISO along with Vendor based Certified staff.
  • Processes: In the proof of concept and implementation stages we use Kalinda IT Fair Business framework, proven processes with clearly defined responsibilities and consistent quality control, that ensures transparency for Kalinda IT’s project teams and our customers every step of the way.
  • Partnerships: We partner with the leading technology manufacturers like Cisco, Microsoft, Verkada, VastData, HP, Dell, Checkpoint, Juniper, Hitachi, Veritas, Trend Micro, Fortinet.

As a result, we are able to work with client-specific requirements and offer solutions tailored to your needs.

We believe constant innovation around IT services can help you simplify, save money and deliver value to your customer. We focus on delivering a full range of services including systems integration, managed services, custom platform builds, DC management and software application development.
