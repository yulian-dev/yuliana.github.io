Our Indigenous Commitment

“We acknowledge the Traditional Owners of the land where we work and live. We pay our respects to Elders past, present and emerging. We celebrate the stories, culture and traditions of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Elders of all communities who also work and live on this land.”

We know what makes IT services and projects successful — and our clients benefit from the high-quality standards we’ve set for ourselves. Our consultants, project managers, system engineers and technicians provide tool-based management to ensure that we deliver exactly what our customers need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire Indigenous youth, emboldening them to be the best versions of themselves.

Our Vision

Build a pathway for the creation of intergeneration wealth, inspire and empower every indigenous person.

Our Approach

Providing education, employment and learning for Indigenous people within the IT, IoT and Cyber Security sectors.

"We need to be focused on action, not simply words. Employment, empowerment and financial freedom should define the indigenous people from every MOB and Nation within Australia"

Michael Dickerson, CEO and Founder
Michael is a proud Wajarri-Nanda man “Desert meets Sea”, from Yamatji Country. With more than 20 years in the Technology industry, he is focused on bridging the wealth, employment shortage that effects many Indigenous people across our country today.
This wealth of experience on working with Large Scale implementation and delivery projects, has allowed him to bring together a rich team of People, Vendors, Partners and Customers who are also focused on making real change in the individuals, families and communities in which they live.

We believe constant innovation around IT services can help you simplify, save money and deliver value to your customer. We focus on delivering a full range of services including systems integration, managed services, custom platform builds, DC management and software application development.
